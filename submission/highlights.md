# Highlights

- Reproducible predictive-autoscaling benchmark built only on public Alibaba traces.
- Aggregate, machine-level, transfer, and service-level evidence are evaluated jointly.
- Linear regression is the strongest average point baseline on aggregate and machine cohorts.
- UA-MSTCN-Lite remains useful by supplying calibrated upper quantiles for control.
- A promoted 139-service official cohort shows service-level headlines are selection-sensitive.
- Predictive control raises mean SLA violation and scaling actions on the broad service cohort.
